What Each Type Does
Magic (Green)

Unlocks offensive and defensive spells. Levels up as you use it in combat, unlocking stronger tiers: Fire becomes Fira then Firaga. Your elemental damage engine.

Fire / Fira / Firaga Blizzard line Thunder line Healing Comet
Support (Blue)

Must be placed in a paired slot alongside another materia. Powers up or modifies the linked materia. Your most precious and limited resource - choose pairings carefully.

Elemental Magnify HP Absorption MP Absorption
Command (Yellow)

Unlocks new non-magic battle actions and special abilities. These don't chain to other materia. Each unlocks a discrete skill or group of skills.

Assess Steal Enemy Skill Refocus
Complete / Independent (Purple)

Passive stat boosters. Equip and forget - they're always working. No activation needed. Stack multiples for compounding bonuses where copies allow.

HP Up MP Up Magic Up First Strike ATB Stagger
Summon (Red)

Weapon slots only - one per character. Summons powerful entities to fight alongside you. Earn them by completing Chadley's shrine challenges in each region.

Ifrit Shiva Ramuh Bahamut Odin

This is the most important page in the guide. Most materia can be purchased or won multiple times. These cannot. Plan your party loadouts around these limits - you cannot give everyone the same build.
