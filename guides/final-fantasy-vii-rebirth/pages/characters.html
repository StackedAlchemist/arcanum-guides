Characters

Switching Roles: You control Cloud by default but can switch to any active party member mid-combat with L2 + directional. Mastering each character's unique mechanic dramatically increases damage output and survivability.
Cloud Strife
Ex-SOLDIER Mercenary
Attacker Flexible

The primary protagonist and default controlled character. Versatile fighter who excels in both physical and magic-enhanced builds. Two stance modes give him exceptional adaptability in any encounter.

Unique Mechanics
Operator Mode Default stance. Faster ATB charge, wider slash arcs, good for aggressive pressure.
Punisher Mode Hold Triangle. Auto-counters melee attacks with massive damage. Slower movement — read enemy patterns first.
Limit: Omnislash / Finishing Touch Omnislash ver. 5 hits multiple times. Use after stagger for peak damage windows.

Barret Wallace
AVALANCHE Leader, Gunner
Ranged Tank

Long-range powerhouse with a gun-arm that deals consistent damage from safety. Best character for building ATB on airborne or hard-to-reach enemies. His massive HP pool makes him the default tank.

Unique Mechanics
Overcharge Charged shot that deals heavy stagger-bar damage. Hold Square to charge, release for a punishing blast.
Lifesaver Passive — generates ATB when any ally takes damage. Keep Barret on field during dangerous phases.
Limit: Catastrophe Unloads a massive barrage on a single target. Best used mid-stagger on bosses.

Tifa Lockhart
Martial Artist, Seventh Heaven
Melee Combo

Highest physical damage potential in the game when her glove mechanic is fully exploited. Requires timing and combo discipline — the skill ceiling is the highest of any character, but the payoff is unmatched burst.

Unique Mechanics
Unbridled Strength Each use powers up her gloves (1→2→3 charges). At max charge, Square attacks deal devastating damage with unique animations.
Rise and Fall / Whirlwind Kick ATB abilities extend combos. Always spend ATB to extend before the combo window closes.
Limit: Final Heaven Massive single-target physical hit. Deals the most raw damage of any physical Limit Break.

Aerith Gainsborough
Ancient, Cetra
Magic Support Healer

The most MP-efficient healer and highest-ceiling magic caster. Her Wards transform the battlefield — placing one before a boss encounter fundamentally changes the difficulty. Never play without her on Hard Mode.

Unique Mechanics
Temperate Ward ATB ability: places a glowing circle. Spells cast inside it are powered up or gain AoE. Critical for MP efficiency.
Ray of Judgment Charged magic beam — hold L1 + Square to charge. Enormous damage with no MP cost once charged.
Limit: Planet's Protection / Healing Wind Planet's Protection: party-wide damage immunity for ~10s. Essential for Hard Mode phase transitions.

Red XIII
Nanaki, Guardian of Cosmo Canyon
Balanced Counter

Unique counter-play character who gets dramatically stronger by taking damage. His Vengeance gauge rewards aggressive positioning and punishes passive play. Scales unusually well into Hard Mode.

Unique Mechanics
Vengeance Gauge Fills when Red XIII takes hits. At full, he enters Vengeance Mode — massively boosted damage and speed.
Stardust Ray His best ATB skill. High stagger damage, especially useful on armored enemies resistant to physical hits.
Limit: Bloodfang / Cosmo Memory Cosmo Memory: hits the entire enemy party. One of the best multi-target Limit Breaks in the game.

Yuffie Kisaragi
Wutai Ninja, Materia Hunter
Ninja Ninjutsu Ranged/Melee

The most technical character and arguably the highest DPS in the game when mastered. Her dual-stance system (thrown shuriken = ranged, retrieved = melee) combined with elemental Ninjutsu makes her a matchup specialist.

Unique Mechanics
Ninjutsu (Triangle) Charges and throws her weapon as a projectile. Allows melee stance while the weapon is thrown. Hold Triangle to set elemental type via Doppelganger.
Art of War ATB ability. Multiplies her next Ninjutsu hit. Always pair with enemy elemental weakness.
Limit: Bloodbath / All Creation All Creation hits all enemies and deals elemental damage — one of the most visually striking and powerful Limits.

Cait Sith
Reeve's Operative, Toy Maker
Support Wildcard

The wild card. His slot machine mechanic introduces controlled chaos — good rolls can be game-changing. His Moogle companion enables unique crowd control and his Fortune Telling sets up party buffs. Underrated in Hard Mode due to ATB generation.

Unique Mechanics
Moogle Ride his giant stuffed mog for tanky, powerful attacks. Separate HP bar — when it breaks, dismounts automatically.
Dice / Toy Soldiers Command abilities. Toy Soldiers summons a squad that auto-attacks — free damage while you play other characters.
Limit: Slots Spin reels for effects ranging from Instant KO to full heal to giant summon. Stop manually or let it auto-resolve.

Key Concepts

Combat Fundamentals

Mechanic How It Works Priority
Stagger Fill enemy's stagger bar by hitting with attacks. Once staggered, enemy takes 160%+ damage for ~10 seconds. Spend ATB and Limit Breaks here. Critical
ATB Gauge Fills by attacking. Spend it on abilities, spells, or items. Two segments max — spend before they overflow. Switch to characters with full ATB. Critical
Pressure Hit enemy's specific weakness (elemental or physical type). Enemy enters "Pressured" state — stagger bar fills faster. High
Synergy Skills Pairs of characters can activate Synergy attacks together. These deal massive damage and are free (no ATB cost). Learn which pairs have synergy. High
Folio (Skill Trees) Each character has a Folio unlocked with Skill Points. Prioritize active abilities over passive nodes early on. Medium
Assess Always Assess new enemies. Reveals weaknesses, immunities, and the specific pressure condition. Required for efficient play. Always
