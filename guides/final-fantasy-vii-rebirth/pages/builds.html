Builds

Build Philosophy: Pair offensive magic materia with Elemental on weapons to add elemental damage to regular attacks. On Hard Mode, MP conservation is everything — equip MP Up, use Magnify+Cure sparingly, and keep ATB Boost on Aerith for emergency revivals. Limited-copy materia goes to your main damage dealer first.
