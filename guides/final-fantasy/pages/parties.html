Team Composition

Party Setup

Party Rules: Cloud is always in your active party (story lock). You pick the other two. The bench character is not completely inactive — they still receive XP and the inactive character passive ability still works. Always check synergy pairs before locking in your lineup.
Recommended Setups

Party Compositions

Standard Meta
Best all-around team — works for 90% of content
Cloud Primary physical DPS, player-controlled
Aerith Healer, magic damage, Ward support
Barret Ranged DPS, tank, Magnify heals

The safest and most forgiving lineup. Aerith keeps everyone alive, Barret provides consistent ranged damage and can carry Magnify+Cure to free Aerith for offense. Cloud deals physical damage in Punisher Mode.

Weakness: Not the highest ceiling. Doesn't specialize into any one damage type.

Magic Nuke
Peak burst damage for weak-to-magic bosses
Cloud Hybrid — Elemental weapon for magic-physical
Aerith Primary magic DPS through Wards
Yuffie Elemental Ninjutsu, Art of War combo

Aerith sets Wards, both she and Yuffie cast inside them for massive amplified magic hits. Yuffie's Ninjutsu costs no MP so she's the sustainable damage. Cloud fills with Elemental weapon. Best against bosses with clear elemental weaknesses.

Weakness: Fragile. No dedicated healer. Requires aggression and good dodge timing.

Burst Physical
Fastest stagger-to-death window in the game
Cloud Punisher Mode, stagger anchor
Tifa Max Unbridled Strength → unload on stagger
Barret ATB battery, ranged pressure, backup heals

Cloud and Tifa together have devastating synergy attacks. Get Tifa's gloves to max charge while Cloud pressures, then both unload during stagger. Barret keeps ATB flowing with Lifeline. Synergy skills between Cloud and Tifa are some of the strongest in the game.

Weakness: Needs Tifa setup time. Vulnerable if stagger is contested or interrupted.

Hard Mode Standard
MP-efficient, survivable, complete toolkit
Cloud Punisher Mode, self-sustain via block
Aerith Planet's Protection, Raise, Ward damage
Barret Magnify+Cure, HP pool, ranged safety

On Hard Mode, no items can be used. Aerith's Planet's Protection and Raise are mandatory survival tools. Barret's Magnify+Cure handles party-wide healing. Cloud generates ATB by blocking in Punisher Mode — free ATB to feed the team.

Weakness: Slightly lower DPS ceiling than offensive parties. Takes longer to kill some bosses.

AoE Clear
Best for trash mobs and multi-enemy encounters
Cloud Whirling Slash to hit all enemies
Aerith Magnify + elemental for group wipes
Red XIII Cosmo Memory Limit, Stardust Ray multi-hit

Magnify+elemental on Aerith nukes every enemy on screen. Cloud's wide slashes hit multiple targets. Red XIII's Cosmo Memory Limit hits all enemies and is free. Best party for grinding mobs and multi-enemy boss phases.

Weakness: Loses single-target efficiency against solo bosses.

ATB Engine
Maximum ATB generation for ability spam
Cloud Precision Defense Focus — ATB from blocks
Barret Lifeline — ATB when allies take damage
Cait Sith Fastest baseline ATB gen in the game

This party generates ATB so fast you can spam abilities constantly. Cloud generates from blocks, Barret from taking hits, Cait from his passive. Toy Soldiers provides free passive DPS while you control other members.

Weakness: Lower peak damage. Best as a support/defensive strategy, not a pure DPS race.

Combat Synergies

Synergy Skills & Abilities

How Synergy Works: When two eligible characters are in the same party, they can activate Synergy Skills and Synergy Abilities. Synergy Skills trigger automatically during regular ATB command combos — no additional input needed. Synergy Abilities are ATB actions that require both characters to act simultaneously. Both deal no ATB cost from the triggering character.
Cloud + Tifa
Dual Strike: combined physical attack that deals heavy damage and builds stagger. One of the strongest synergy combos — a primary reason to run Cloud/Tifa together.
Cloud + Aerith
Arcane Ward: Aerith empowers Cloud's attacks with magical energy. Doubles ATB generation when Cloud attacks from inside a Ward. Essential for Ward-based builds.
Cloud + Barret
Steelskin / Focused Shot combo: Barret boosts Cloud's defense while laying down ranged fire. Good for tanking dangerous phases while maintaining pressure.
Tifa + Aerith
Fleeting Familiar: conjures a spectral companion that mirrors Tifa's attacks. Effectively doubles Tifa's hit count during combo windows — dramatic damage increase.
Barret + Tifa
Overrun: Barret launches Tifa toward a target for a massive aerial slam. Good for aerial enemies and launching stagger combos. Generates pressure state on most enemies.
Yuffie + Cloud
Windstorm follow-up: when Cloud knocks enemies airborne, Yuffie's aerial ninjutsu deals bonus damage. Excellent for airborne combat phases.
Red XIII + Aerith
Cosmo Memory Ward: Aerith's Ward amplifies Red XIII's Vengeance Mode attacks. Best way to maximize Red XIII's burst — stack Vengeance inside an Aerith Ward.
Cait Sith + Any
Cait's Toy Soldiers will automatically support any partner's attack patterns. Less specific synergy, but Cait's Fortune Telling provides party-wide stat buffs that benefit all pairs.
Limited Copies

Who Gets the Limited Gear

Limited Materia: Some materia only has 1 copy in the game and cannot be duplicated until very late. Assign these wisely. The list below reflects single-run priority — if you're doing a second playthrough, you can spread these more freely.
Materia / Item Best Recipient Priority Reasoning
ATB Boost Cloud or Aerith S Cloud for stagger windows. Aerith for emergency Planet's Protection on Hard Mode. Second copy goes to Tifa for burst extension.
Magnify Barret S Barret + Magnify + Cure is the best passive healing setup in the game. Frees Aerith entirely for damage and utility.
Elemental (Weapon) Cloud or Yuffie S On weapon, adds chosen element to all physical attacks. Cloud for consistent physical/magic hybrid. Yuffie if she's your DPS primary.
Elemental (Armor) Aerith A On armor, halves/nullifies chosen element. Aerith is the most likely to be targeted by elemental attacks. Protects your healer.
Warding Aerith A Paired with Subversion on armor, halves MP cost of status spells. Essential on Hard Mode where MP is the primary constraint.
MP Absorb Aerith B Restore MP when dealing damage with paired materia. Pairs with Fire/Ice/Lightning for passive MP recovery. Hard Mode lifeline.
HP Absorb Cloud or Tifa B Self-sustain for your main attacker. Cloud in Punisher Mode — generates ATB and heals simultaneously.
Synergy (rare) Your primary pair B Give to the character you control most. Synergy activates from the controlled character's actions — more actions = more synergy.
Gotterdammerung (accessory) Tifa A Starts battle with full Limit Gauge. Tifa's Final Heaven or Cloud's Omnislash are both excellent, but Tifa deals more raw physical damage per Limit during stagger.
