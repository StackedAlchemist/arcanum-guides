The shape of the game

  • Pick a class, level to 70, then graduate to the endgame. The campaign is the tutorial; the real game is the gear chase at max level across escalating difficulty.
  • Power comes from the gear & systems, not raw level. Aspects, Uniques, Tempering, Masterworking, Paragon boards and Glyphs are where a character actually becomes strong.
  • Difficulty is a ladder. Four Standard tiers (Normal, Hard, Expert, Penitent) then Torment I–XII — higher tiers gate the best gear and scale the rewards.
  • It's seasonal & live-service. Every season adds a mechanic and resets the ladder on the Seasonal realm; the build meta rotates with each patch. Treat tier lists as perishable.
  • Eight classes as of Lord of Hatred (April 2026): the five launch classes plus Spiritborn, Paladin, and Warlock.
How to use this guideWe deliberately don't pin a season's "best build" — it would be wrong within a patch. Instead: learn the systems and how a build is assembled here, then plug in the current meta from a live planner (linked on the Builds page). Master the engine and any build makes sense.
