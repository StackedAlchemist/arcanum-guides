The Loop

The Endgame

Once the campaign ends and you hit max level, Diablo IV opens into a menu of activities — each one farms a different reward. Knowing what each gives you is how you turn aimless grinding into a build coming together.

07 · The Overworld

Open-world farming

  • Helltides — corrupted zones that rotate across the map; kill for Aberrant Cinders and open Helltide chests for targeted gear. The bread-and-butter farm, especially fresh into endgame.
  • World Bosses — scheduled overworld giants; quick group kills for guaranteed rares/legendaries and materials.
  • Legion Events & World Events — timed map events with crowds of monsters; efficient XP and loot when you pass one.
  • The Tree of Whispers — complete Whisper objectives across the map for Grim Favors, then cash them in for choose-your-slot Caches. Excellent targeted gearing.

Match the activity to the need. Need Paragon power? Run Nightmare Dungeons / the Pit for Glyph XP. Need to finish an item? Push the Pit for Masterworking mats. Need a specific slot? Tree of Whispers caches or Undercity Tributes. Drowning in nothing? You're probably farming the wrong activity for your goal.
09 · The Systems

Mercenaries, War Plans & Seasons

  • Mercenaries (VoH) — recruit a hireling to fight beside you, plus a second as a Reinforcement that triggers on a condition. They add damage, utility, and a bit of build flexibility.
  • War Plans (LoH) — a structured endgame playlist: assemble a route of activities to run, progress Activity Skill Trees, and earn extra loot and rewards along the way. The new spine that ties the endgame activities together.
  • Seasons & Realms — the live-service core. Each season (roughly quarterly) adds a temporary mechanic and a Season Journey, on a fresh Seasonal realm where everyone starts over (your permanent characters live on the Eternal realm). A Battle Pass runs alongside.
  • How to climb Torment I→XII: gear up and cap defenses on your current tier until it feels trivial, then step up one. Don't skip tiers chasing loot you can't yet survive to collect — efficiency beats ego.
Live-service realitySeasonal mechanics, exact drop sources, and which activity is "most efficient" shift every patch. The structure above is stable; for the current season's mechanic and the optimal farm, check the planners linked on the Builds page.
