The Engine

Gear & Power Systems

In Diablo IV your character level matters far less than what you do to your gear. These are the systems that actually turn a level-70 nobody into a Torment-shredding monster — learn them once and every build clicks into place.

04 · The Loot

Rarities, tiers & affixes

RarityWhat it is
Magic (blue)1–2 affixes. Leveling fodder; salvage for materials.
Rare (yellow)More affixes; the base you'll imprint Aspects onto early.
Legendary (orange)Carries a Legendary Aspect (a build-altering power). The backbone of most builds.
Unique (brown/amber)Fixed item with a unique power that can't be crafted — often defines a build.
Mythic UniqueThe rarest, build-defining chase items (formerly "Uber Uniques") with multiple Legendary powers built in.
  • Item tiers: Normal → Sacred → Ancestral. Ancestral gear (unlocked in Torment) has the highest stat ceiling and can roll Greater Affixes — supercharged versions of an affix. Endgame gearing is a hunt for Ancestral items with the right Greater Affixes.
  • Affixes are what you actually want. A great item base with the wrong affixes is junk; learn the 3–4 stats your build needs and read items by those.
05 · The Craft

Aspects, Tempering, Masterworking & Runewords

Four crafting systems stack onto a piece of gear. Together they take a raw drop and finish it into an endgame item.

SystemWhat it doesWhere
Aspects & the Codex of PowerImprint a Legendary power onto a Rare/Legendary. The Codex stores a permanent copy of Aspects you've unlocked (from dungeons or by extracting drops), so you can re-apply your build's key powers.Occultist
TemperingAdd up to two extra tempered affixes from a manual you've learned — big, build-specific stats. Each item has limited reroll charges, so commit carefully.Blacksmith
MasterworkingUpgrade an item's affixes in ranks (to 12); ranks 4, 8 and 12 crit, hugely boosting a random affix. The Pit feeds the materials.Blacksmith
RunewordsSocket a Rune of Ritual (condition) + Rune of Invocation (effect) to trigger powerful scripted effects — a flexible second layer of build power.Drops / socketed gear
Gems & socketsSlot gems for flat stats/resists; a smaller but reliable boost.Jeweler
The finishing order on a chase item: imprint the right Aspect → Temper the affixes your build needs → then sink Masterworking materials to push (and try to crit the stat that matters most). Don't fully Masterwork an item you'll replace next week.
05b · Lord of Hatred

Horadric Cube, Talismans & Charms

Lord of Hatred adds a third crafting layer on top of Aspects/Tempering/Masterworking:

SystemWhat it does
Horadric CubeAdd, change, or remove affixes on gear using Horadric materials — more surgical than imprinting, with its own material economy.
Talismans & CharmsEquip Talismans that roll set-style bonuses when paired with matching Charms. A build-defining layer separate from Legendary Aspects.
Skill tree reworkLoH removed standalone Key Passives from the skill tree. Builds now hinge on skill variants, branches, and the class mechanic — not a single locked key passive node.
06 · The Ladder

Paragon, levels & difficulty

  • Level cap is 70 (raised from 60 in Lord of Hatred) — that's where you finish spending Skill Points.
  • Paragon is the long game. Now decoupled from character level, Paragon points feed into Paragon Boards — grids of stats, magic nodes, and Glyph sockets. Glyphs level up (largely via the Pit) to amplify the nodes around them. This is most of your endgame power growth.
  • Difficulty is a ladder you climb. Four Standard tiers — Normal, Hard, Expert, Penitent — then Torment I–XII. Torment unlocks Ancestral gear and the toughest, most rewarding content; each step up scales monster level and loot quality.
  • Resistances & Armor matter at high Torment. Cap your resistances and hit the Armor cap before pushing — survivability, not just damage, gates how high you climb.
The progression in one lineLevel to 70 → reach Torment for Ancestral gear → farm the right Greater-Affix items → finish them with Aspects/Tempering/Masterworking → grind Paragon & Glyphs → climb Torment tiers and the Pit. That loop is the endgame.
