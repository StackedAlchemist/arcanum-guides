The Cast

Characters & Roles

FF IV's party is a revolving door of named heroes, each locked to one role. You rarely choose who's in the line-up — the story does — so the skill is getting the most from whoever you currently have, and stripping gear off anyone about to leave.

05 · The Turn

Cecil: Dark Knight → Paladin

Cecil's transformation is the story's spine and a real mechanical shift. As a Dark Knight he wields Darkness (damage at the cost of his own HP) and heavy dark gear. At Mount Ordeals he renounces that power and becomes a Paladin: he loses Darkness but gains Cover (intercept hits for weak allies), holy resistance, and access to the best knightly gear — turning him into the durable leader who carries the back half of the game.

Don't over-invest in Dark Knight gear right before Ordeals; the shift re-frames his whole kit toward tanking and protection.
