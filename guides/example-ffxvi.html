Combat & Stagger

XVI is a character-action game wearing a Final Fantasy crest. Damage matters, but stagger matters more. Every notable enemy carries a Will gauge — chip it to zero to Stagger them, opening a window where a climbing damage multiplier lets your biggest abilities erase health bars.

GIF · a clean stagger → Gigaflare dump

The core loop: build Will with cheap abilities, then unload everything the instant the bar breaks.

  • Break with one set, dump with the other. Some abilities shred Will; others hit HP hard. Hold the heavy hitters for the stagger window.
  • Precision Dodge (last-instant R1) slows time and sets up a counter — your best offense is a perfect evade.
  • Magic Burst — tap Magic at the end of a melee string to cancel recovery and fire free damage. Make it muscle memory.
Master's noteHolding a long-cooldown ability for the stagger, instead of firing it on cooldown, is the single biggest skill jump in the game.

The Eikons

Eikons are your classes. Each grants a Feat (a signature move) and two ability slots; you equip three at once and swap mid-combo. Mastering an ability lets you equip it under any Eikon — the whole build game.

The late-game Eikon (story spoiler)

The antagonist wields Ultima, and the finale hands Clive sweeping Ultima powers for their duration — spectacle rather than a permanent build Eikon, so plan loadouts around the eight above.

Mastery Loadouts

A build is your three Eikons, the two abilities under each, and your accessories. Respec is free — tailor it per fight. Three that work:

All-Rounder

  • Garuda · Gouge + Wicked Wheel
  • Bahamut · Gigaflare + Satellite
  • Odin · Dancing Steel + Gungnir

Stagger Nuke

  • Shiva · Diamond Dust
  • Bahamut · Gigaflare
  • Ramuh · Judgment Bolt

Ranged / Flier

  • Ramuh · Blind Justice (Feat)
  • Bahamut · Wings of Light + Satellite
  • Garuda · Gouge

Bosses & Hunts

Fights come in two shapes: grounded action bosses you beat with the stagger loop, and the colossal Eikon-vs-Eikon set-pieces that trade difficulty for spectacle. The optional Hunt Board posts Notorious Marks — your pipeline for rare crafting materials.
