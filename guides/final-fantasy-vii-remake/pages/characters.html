The Party

Characters & Builds

Four playable heroes in the main game (plus Yuffie in INTERmission), each with a unique mechanic. You'll usually field a party of three and swap between them — build around the shared stagger loop, not isolated damage.

04 · The Heroes

Roles & mechanics

CharacterMechanicRole & build
CloudOperator / Punisher modes (Triangle to swap); Punisher counters attacks)Balanced frontline DPS. Operator for mobility, Punisher to shred when the enemy attacks into you. Sword + Elemental, ATB Boost; the all-rounder.
TifaUnbridled Strength upgrades her abilities (Whirling Uppercut → Omnistrike, Rise & Fall → Rear/Front; True Strike)The stagger-damage amplifier. Build chi/Unbridled, then dump True Strike into the stagger window for a huge multiplier. Pure ATK fists.
BarretOvercharge (manual recharge for big ranged hits); can hold aggroRanged DPS / tank. Lifesaver + Steelskin to soak; great against flyers. Fire/Earth + the ranged chip that maintains stagger.
AerithArcane Ward (a zone that double-casts spells cast inside it); rangedCaster / healer / control. Drop Arcane Ward, stand in it, double-cast nukes. Magnify + Healing, Magic Efficiency, high Magic gear.
Yuffie (Intergrade)Throw + elemental Ninjutsu; Synergy with Sonon in INTERmissionFast hybrid; swap elements to Pressure quickly, then Banishment/Doppelganger burst. The star of the Yuffie episode.
The winning pattern (same as Rebirth's roots): build ATB → Pressure with the right element/tactic → break the Stagger → with Tifa's True Strike up and ATB Boost ready, dump Limit Breaks and abilities into the window.
05 · Party Picks

Who to field

  • Cloud + Tifa + Aerith — the default: Cloud/Tifa stagger and amplify, Aerith heals and double-casts magic. Strong everywhere.
  • Cloud + Barret + (Tifa/Aerith) — tankier and good vs flyers; Barret's range keeps pressure on airborne or distant foes.
  • Anyone + Aerith — whenever you want a hard caster/healer and Arcane Ward burst.
  • Story locks the party often — level Materia across everyone (it carries between characters) so a forced solo/duo chapter isn't a wall.
