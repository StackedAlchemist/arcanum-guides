07 · The Arsenal

The twelve sealed weapons & the Knife dilemma

The third world hides the twelve legendary weapons (Excalibur, Ragnarok, Masamune, Apollo's Harp, Holy Lance, Sage's Staff, Assassin's Dagger, Yoichi's Bow, and more) behind the sealed tablets. A few are build-defining: Masamune grants innate Haste and first-strike; Apollo's Harp deals 4× damage to Exdeath; Assassin's Dagger can instant-kill.

Chicken Knife vs Brave Blade. You can only take one. The Brave Blade starts strong but loses power every time you flee a battle; the Chicken Knife grows stronger the more battles you've fled (but can randomly make a character flee). For a Dual-Wield + Rapid Fire run, the Chicken Knife is usually the pick — its ceiling is enormous and a stray flee barely matters.

Pair the legendary weapons with the combos: two of them on a Dual-Wield Freelancer, fired through Rapid Fire, is the most damage in the game; a Masamune on your caster keeps them permanently hasted and acting first.
