The Exploits

Combos, Blue Magic & Best Jobs

The reason FF V's job system is legendary: a few cross-class combinations break the game wide open. Here are the four pillars, the enemy skills worth learning, and the master-first priority list.

04 · The Exploits

Broken combos

  1. Dual-Wield + Rapid Fire — equip two strong weapons (Ninja), then !Rapid Fire (Ranger) for 8 hits that ignore defense and evasion. The premier physical build.
  2. Spellblade + Rapid Fire + Dual-Wield — enchant your weapons with !Spellblade (Flare, Holy, or instant-Death), then Rapid Fire to spray enchanted hits or one-shot whole rooms.
  3. Dualcast nuker — master Red Mage for !Dualcast, slot offensive Black/Summon/Time magic, and fire two Flares / Meteors / Bahamuts per turn. Pre-cast Quick (Time) for four.
  4. !Mix (Chemist) — Goliath Tonic doubles a character's max HP; Dragon Power buffs stats; other mixes nuke or full-revive. Cheap ingredients, huge payoffs.
  5. !Zeninage (Samurai) — throw gil for guaranteed big nonelemental AoE; trivializes crowds if you're rich.
The two pillars: physical carries want Dual-Wield + Rapid Fire (add Spellblade for elements/instakill); magic carries want Dualcast. Master those four jobs (Ninja, Ranger, Mystic Knight, Red Mage) and the back half of the game folds.
05 · Enemy Skills

Blue Magic worth grabbing

Equip Learning (Blue Mage) and survive an enemy casting the spell — or use Beastmaster !Control to force it. Standouts:

  • Mighty Guard — party-wide Protect + Shell (+ Float). One of the best buffs in the game.
  • White Wind — heals the whole party for the caster's current HP. Free, scaling, no MP-heavy cost.
  • Level 5 Death — instantly kills every enemy whose level is a multiple of 5 (works on some bosses — check levels).
  • Aero / Aera / Aeroga — reliable early wind damage before you have Black magic.
  • Goblin Punch, Death Claw, Time Slip, Aqua Breath, Missile — situational gems for control and damage.
06 · Priorities

Best jobs to master

  • Ninja — Dual-Wield is non-negotiable for any physical build.
  • Ranger — Rapid Fire is the best physical command in the game.
  • Mystic Knight — Spellblade turns those hits into elemental/instant-death.
  • Red Mage — Dualcast doubles every nuke; the magic equivalent of Rapid Fire.
  • Monk (early HP), Black / Time / Summon (your caster's spell lists), Chemist (Mix utility), Samurai (Zeninage + Shirahadori).
  • Then settle into Freelancer or Mime with the abilities above stacked.
