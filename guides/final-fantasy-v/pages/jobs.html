The Classes

The 22 Jobs & Ability Engine

FF V's job system is the whole game. You earn jobs from the Crystals, learn their abilities permanently, then bolt one ability from any class onto another. Master the right jobs and the back half folds.

03 · The Engine

The secondary-ability engine

  • Learn it once, keep it forever. ABP unlocks a Job's abilities permanently; switch Jobs freely and slot a learned ability as your secondary.
  • Passives stack onto Freelancer/Mime. Master Ninja and Freelancer keeps Dual-Wield innately; master Monk and it keeps Barehanded/Counter; etc. This is how you assemble a god character.
  • Mime is the combo chassis — three ability slots let you run, say, !Dualcast + !Summon + !Time on one character, all inheriting mastered passives.
  • Don't sleep on cheap masteries: Monk early (HP), Black/White/Time for your casters, then the four pillars on the Combos page.
