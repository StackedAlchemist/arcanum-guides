Unity is the second economy. You spend it on tradition trees, each of which opens an Ascension Perk slot — and those slots gate the single biggest mid-game identity choice: your ascension path.

05 · Doctrine

Traditions & Ascension Perks

Traditions are unity-bought trees (Expansion, Discovery, Domination, Prosperity, Supremacy, Diplomacy…). Each completed tree unlocks an Ascension Perk slot. Slots and picks are deliberately scarce — that scarcity is by design — so choose trees that match your plan.

Ascension Perks are empire-wide bonuses and gates to ascension paths, megastructures, and ambitions. Notable: the megastructure unlocks, the three ascension-path perks, and in 4.4 the reworked Defender of the Galaxy ambition and the Wanderlust nomad perk.

06 · Transcendence

Ascension paths

The big mid-game identity commitment. Pick one lane (dabbling is possible, but going deep pulls heavy ethics attraction):

  • Psionic (Spiritualist-leaning): tap the Shroud for powerful, RNG-flavored bonuses and patrons. High ceiling, some risk.
  • Genetic (biology): heavily mod traits, clone/tailor pops; strong output and adaptability.
  • Cybernetic (Materialist/Spiritualist friendly): cyborg pops, solid all-round bonuses; bridges organic and machine.
  • Synthetic (Materialist): convert pops to synths for top-tier output — the classic robot ascension.
  • Virtuality / Nanotech and newer DLC lanes: Virtuality (a Machine route) is a strong tall/compact play and, per the devs, works well even for Nomads.

No single "best" path — match it to your empire. Synthetic and Genetic are the reliable output kings; Psionic has the flashiest highs.
