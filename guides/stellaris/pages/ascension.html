05 · Doctrine

Traditions & Ascension Perks

Traditions are unity-bought trees (Expansion, Discovery, Domination, Prosperity, Supremacy, Diplomacy…). Each completed tree unlocks an Ascension Perk slot. Slots and picks are deliberately scarce — that scarcity is by design — so choose trees that match your plan.

Ascension Perks are empire-wide bonuses and gates to ascension paths, megastructures, and ambitions. Notable: the megastructure unlocks, the three ascension-path perks, and in 4.4 the reworked Defender of the Galaxy ambition and the Wanderlust nomad perk.

Tradition treeFocusTake it when
ExpansionCheaper/faster colonies & outposts, growthGoing wide — grabbing lots of systems and planets.
DiscoveryResearch speed, survey, anomaliesTech-focused or exploration-heavy starts.
ProsperityProduction, planet capacity, jobsBuilding a strong economic core early.
DominationStability, governance, slavery/edictsWide or authoritarian empires managing sprawl.
SupremacyFleet, weapons, shipbuildingEarly-aggression or conquest plans.
DiplomacyTrust, envoys, federation/tradeFederation builds and diplo/Galactic Community play.
First tree = your plan. Open with the tree that matches your win condition (Expansion for wide, Discovery for tech, Prosperity for economy, Supremacy for early war). Each finished tree is one Ascension Perk slot — and those slots are deliberately scarce, so don't pad trees you don't need.
06 · Transcendence

Ascension paths

The big mid-game identity commitment. Pick one lane (dabbling is possible, but going deep pulls heavy ethics attraction):

PathFitsPayoff
PsionicSpiritualist-leaningTap the Shroud for powerful, RNG-flavored bonuses and patrons. High ceiling, some risk — the flashiest highs.
GeneticAny (biology)Heavily mod traits, clone and tailor pops per job; superb output and adaptability.
CyberneticAny (biology)Cyborg pops with strong all-round bonuses; bridges organic and machine, flexible.
SyntheticMaterialistConvert pops to synths for top-tier output — the classic robot ascension and an output king.
VirtualityMachine / tall empiresVirtual pops for a strong compact play (works well for Nomads, per the devs).
NanotechMachine AgeDistinct machine-ascension path with nanite-focused bonuses — separate from Virtuality.

No single "best" path — match it to your empire. Synthetic and Genetic are the reliable output kings; Psionic has the flashiest highs. Commit by mid-game and steer your research toward the path's techs.
