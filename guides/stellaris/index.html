The core loop — the four X's

Stellaris runs on four pillars: eXplore, eXpand, eXploit, eXterminate.

  • Explore with Science Ships. Survey systems for deposits, anomalies, and archaeology sites. Early exploration sets up everything.
  • Expand by building Outposts (Starbases) to claim systems, then colonizing habitable worlds inside your borders. You can only colonize within your own territory.
  • Exploit by working planets: pops fill jobs, jobs produce resources (energy, minerals, food, alloys, research, unity, consumer goods, strategic resources).
  • Exterminate — or out-tech, out-grow, out-diplomacy. Victory can be military, but tall economic/tech empires win too.

Early game is exploration and expansion; mid game is consolidation and tech; late game is megastructures and surviving the end-game Crisis.
