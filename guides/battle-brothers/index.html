How to read this Codex

The Tier marks

Builds and weapons carry a rating from the company's own ledger:

S meta-defining, always good
A strong, reliable pick
B situational, good in a role
C niche or early-only
D bring something else

One rule above all

Battle Brothers punishes the careless and rewards the patient. A retreat that keeps your company alive beats a victory that costs you two veterans you spent forty days raising.

Death is permanent. Plan like it.
