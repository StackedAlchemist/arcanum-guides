How to read this manual

The Tier marks

Recipes, buildings, and strategies carry a rating from a pioneer's ledger:

S always worth it, build it every time
A strong, reliable pick
B good in a specific role
C situational or early-only
D skip it, there's a better way

One rule above all

Satisfactory rewards the patient planner. A line built at clean whole-number ratios runs forever untouched; a line thrown together "to fix later" becomes the spaghetti you tear down at 3am.

Plan the ratio first. Lay the belt second.
