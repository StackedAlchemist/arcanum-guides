The New Edition

Enhanced Version Features (v1.5.0)

The Ivalice Chronicles Enhanced version layers New Game+, a Zodiac viewer, and a stack of quality-of-life on top of the classic War of the Lions content — and still ships a Classic version for purists.

11 · The New Edition

What's new in Enhanced

  • New Game+: start a fresh run carrying over unit levels, item data, and more from a completed playthrough.
  • Zodiac Compatibility viewer: check any unit's sign and compatibility from the status screen (no more guessing the hidden multiplier — see Combat).
  • Status under the cursor: inspect the unit under the cursor while picking a move/target tile.
  • "Remove All Equipment" button and clearer job-unlock condition display on the status screen.
  • Battle camera angle/zoom is retained for the whole battle.
  • New toggles: Maintain Auto-proceed (cutscenes), Ability Incantations Guaranteed, and Ability Cursor Memory.
  • Full English and Japanese voice acting, added text support (Simplified/Traditional Chinese, Korean), and stability improvements.
For puristsThe package also includes a Classic version, which received its own smaller fixes (errand-report bug fix, sound tweaks).
