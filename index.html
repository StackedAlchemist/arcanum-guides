The Arcanum

An arcane library struck by lightning — deep guides for the games worth mastering.

I grew up on Mario RPG, Joust and Rampage, cut my teeth on Brood War macro and Diablo loot, raided in WoW, and now lose weekends to Factorio trains and Satisfactory belts. I love deep systems — the kind that reward a tactician who reads the machine and bends it.

The Arcanum is where I forge that obsession into mastery guides: no padding, no SEO sludge, just the builds, the math, and the secrets the tutorials never tell you. If you play to master, not just to pass the time — this is your library.
